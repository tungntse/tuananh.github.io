ABOUT F-CAMP

How far is K15 ready to explode with F-camp 2019? A very strong comeback to welcome new students has been carefully prepared by the Organizing Committee, promising to include a full combo: happy - healthy - quality - emotional explosion. F-Camp is the most welcomed annual event in Hoa Lac each school season. This is also a gift from previous students - under the companionship and advisor of the International Cooperation and Personal Development Department - IC-PDP for new course students. F-Camp is not just a festival, but also a continuation of the culture and the giving of values ​​that form the "soul" of FPT University and FPT students. Attracting from the "back to school" days, F camp 2019 was good with the concept of "Justice League". Set in the League of Justice, after the attack of Steffenwolf, the Earth has returned to peace and holds 3 boxes of Mother Boxes. However, just recently, Steff sent a provocative letter to Earth to retrieve the 3 boxes and invade the Earth. Receiving that news, 4 races: Atlantis (village P), Greenlantern (village F), Amazon clan (village T), and Human race (U village) have allied together ... promising to bring a lot of emotions. k15. Every season Fcamp, the BTC team, OGL members always try to bring out the most unique, new, and most calm things, and do not forget to give their love to students of the new course. F camp is an indispensable specialty of F school, unlike other schools, FPT University has a very distinctive way of welcoming new students, but also brings many meanings. The program lasts for one day, k15 will welcome the maturity ceremony, have fun, make friends between generations, understand FPT culture to quickly integrate into learning as well as life. In Hoa Lac, through a series of exciting and exciting activities, it promises to bring about unfamiliar impressions in the student days. F-Camp 2019 will take place on November 3, let's see who the village leaders this year are.
